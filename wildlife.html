LIFE IN THE WILD

Have you ever thought how life in the wild be like?

From the minute insects to giant cats and bears of the forest.
From the herds of zebras and gigantic elephants to the king of animals Lion of the grasslands.
From the tiny krill to the humongous blue whale in the vast blue ocean.
From fresh water crabs to ferocious strong jawwed crocodile raging in freshwaters.
From crawling scorpions and slithering snakes to the humping camels in sun kissed deserts.
From the seals at the shore to the penguins at the poles.
From the furry sheep to heavy and gregarious yaks in the mountains.
Every part of the world is running with life and the wild is full of it.
